Building a cross-border beauty ecosystem across content, commerce, community and paid growth.

MarketUS / EU
Period2022—2023
RoleOnline Brand Marketing Manager
FocusBrand / E-commerce / Meta

Context

Lavislash is a beauty and lash brand serving international customers through an e-commerce-led model.

My role

  • Brand and content direction
  • Website and e-commerce operation
  • Campaign planning and creative briefing
  • Meta Ads operation and reporting
  • Team collaboration and media approval

The challenge

Turn separate channels and product listings into one connected customer journey that builds trust, educates buyers and supports daily revenue.

Strategy

Define a clear role for every channel: website for discovery and conversion; social for awareness, education and community; paid media for acquisition; content for trust and repeated demand.
